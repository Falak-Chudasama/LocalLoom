Meet Artisan - Nilesh Agrawal

Meet Nilesh Agrawal, a master potter from Gujarat with over 20 years of
craftsmanship. Blending traditional Gujarati motifs with contemporary
designs, he creates hand-painted terracotta pots and eco-friendly clay
dinnerware using locally sourced materials. His work, showcased at Rann
Utsav and national craft fairs, reflects a deep commitment to heritage
and sustainability. Explore his handcrafted pottery and bring home a
piece of Indian artistry!
